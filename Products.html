Ginger

This is the main traded ginger in Nepal. It is collected, sorted and may be peeled before eating. Both mature and immature ginger roots are consumed as a fresh vegetable in cut or chopped form. In the food processing large quantities of fresh ginger are used in the preparation of sauce and pickles. Ginger is used in various forms such as fresh, dried, sliced, ground, preserved salted, pickled crystallized and candied ginger. In it’s processing ginger oil is first obtained. Afterwards the processed ginger is dried, sliced or pulverized to ginger powder used as condiment/ingredient in various consuming industries.
Ginger is loaded with antioxidants, compounds that prevent stress and damage to your body’s DNA. They help your body fight off chronic diseases like high blood pressure, heart disease, and diseases of the lungs, plus promote healthy aging.

Orange

Oranges is also seasonable fruits and it is most popular in the mountain region of Nepal, there are many Health Benefits of orange, it helps to prevent the cancer, kidney diseases and many more other disease. Oranges are effective in both preventing and treating certain diseases like bronchitis, pneumonia, tuberculosis and rheumatism. Orange fruit is a small evergreen flowering plants growing to about 5 to 8 meters tall and Fruit bearing begins at three to six weeks. Oranges is not only delicious and juicy but also contains an impressive list of essential nutrients, vitamins, minerals for normal growth and development.

Lemons

Lemon farming is most popular in the Mid-Hills of Nepal, As a citrus fruit, lemons can offer many health benefits to human beings Lemons are commonly known their high content of vitamin C, which is important for many bodily functions including: maintaining healthy bones and teeth and Lemons are also rich in other essential vitamins and minerals such as potassium, calcium and magnesium, as well as antioxidants, which are important for fighting diseases and healing wounds. Lemons are an excellent choice of crop for Nepali farmers as they have many uses, such as: juice, fragrance, flavor, cleaning and health benefits.

Avocado

The avocado is usually consumed fresh as a fruit or as an ingredient in salads or savory dishes. It has a markedly higher fat content than other fruits and is a staple in diets that have limited access to foods with high contents of monounsaturated fats. It is the main ingredient in guacamole, a popular Mexican dip. In Asia, avocados are used in desserts and dessert drinks.
