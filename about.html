Our Company

Sfour Agro Farm Private Limited Has basically established us a corporate institution for the business purpose. It provide top quality agro base export and import services in the present changing context of old and surroundings. We are starting our journey to achieve a platform in the world by Produce Nepali natural organic spices and distribute it in the national & international market. Our company is registered on Government of Nepal Ministry of Industry, Commerce & Supplies Office of the Company Registrar, and we are trying to make our best position in produce & refining good quality spices. We are able to provide our products item to any destination in the world. We also deliver customer satisfaction with honesty, sincerity and gratitude. We offer a full range of products that are essential to today’s human health environment, our main goal is to provide high quality products, spices without chemicals and services to customer’s requirements.

Over the past 3 years we have been supplying authenticated and organic agriculture products to over 500 valued customers. We are focused on helping people from remote part of Nepal by generating economic activities and changing there lifestyle. Further in order to provide better products we are tied up with more than 50 Agriculture based cooperative organization all over Nepal.

S. Four Agro Farm Private limited is leading Agriculture based import and export oriented company located at Sainamaina 3, Rupandehi, Lumbuni Province, Nepal. S.Four Agro Farm has been dedicated to supply the best quality agriculture products which are available remote part of Nepal and delivering it to any destination whole over the world.requirements.
